About Us

Who we are

We are Knightri Triathlon Team. A new generation of athletes.

Its about what’s inside you, its not about the finish line, or the award, or the social media, or what anyone else thinks, its about what’s inside you and how deep you want to dig to find out.

Years of experience, months of preparation each season, good days to come, minutes to think it through, seconds till you join Us!

What can we offer

The Knightri Team aims to provide members with the latest information on triathlon performance related topics.

We share our knowledge, experience and strategies enabling Team members to achieve their training, racing or lifestyle goals. We also always strive to make the journey more rewarding and fun.
